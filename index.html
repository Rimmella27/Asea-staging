About
Asëa
ah-SAY-ah

Asëа is a personal health intelligence system for you and your family. It tracks what you eat, drink, and feel — then quietly watches for the patterns your body already knows.

Log disappears into the background. When there’s something worth saying, Asëа surfaces it.

The name

The name traces to the Latin and Italian root asea — related to agio, meaning ease, comfort, and freedom from constraint. It’s the feeling of a body that isn’t fighting itself.

And for a bit of fun — in Quenya, Tolkien’s invented language, asëа also carries the sense of ease and quiet restoration. The diaeresis over the e marks it as two syllables: ah-SAY-ah. A small detail that rewards a little attention.
